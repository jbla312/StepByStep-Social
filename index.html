Real-World Generalization

Many students excel in a controlled classroom setting but struggle to apply social cues in public. Our program solves this by focusing on Community-Based Instruction (CBI).

  • Small Group Ratio: Individualized attention for every child.
  • Morning Workshops: Direct instruction on greetings, safety, and money management.
  • Afternoon Outings: Trips to parks, cafes, and stores to practice skills.

Program Highlights

Social Interaction

Guided peer-to-peer communication and cooperative play strategies.

Community Safety

Navigating public spaces, identifying safety signs, and stranger awareness.

Daily Living

Ordering food, handling small transactions, and requesting help.
